About Atara

I've been a licensed massage therapist since 2004 - almost 22 years! I love what I do. Each session is custom-made to meet you exactly where you are, with just the right pressure and focus for your unique needs. Whether you're seeking relief from chronic pain, support for emotional regulation, or simply a peaceful moment to relax and reset, I'm here to create a safe, supportive space for your healing journey.

Services

Thoughtful care tailored to your needs

Massage Therapy

With nearly 22 years of experience, I meet each client where they are with customized massage sessions. Whether you want to focus on your upper body, neck and head, feet and legs, or enjoy a full body massage, you design your preferences. Each massage is tailored to your needs with just the right pressure for your muscles.

Benefits: Supports your circulatory and lymphatic systems, improves mood, and helps with carpal tunnel syndrome, sciatica, headaches, regular aches and pains, or simply provides a relaxing treat.

Craniosacral Therapy

A more advanced therapy using a softer touch with deeper intention to benefit your nervous system and emotional regulation. Craniosacral therapy helps you regain equilibrium physically, emotionally, and mentally. It trusts the inner wisdom of each individual to heal from within.

Helps with: Headaches, migraines, chronic pain, brain fog, vertigo, digestive issues, emotional symptoms, and when you just don't feel like yourself. For babies and children, it addresses colic, reflux, tongue tie, constipation, developmental milestones, behavior management, and emotional regulation.

Reflexology

Addresses all systems of the body through massage and pressure points on the hands, feet, and lower legs. This therapy is wonderful for deep relaxation and balancing the body's systems.

Effective for: Chronic symptoms that are difficult to treat medically, such as eczema and psoriasis, as well as promoting overall wellness and relaxation.
